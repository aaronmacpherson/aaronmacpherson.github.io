About me

So lets start off with a little bit of personal background. I was born and raised in Nova Scotia until 2004 when we moved to Ontario. I've always had a passing interest in technology but never pursued it in education until recently. I spent most of my high school years and early college years studying in several business subjects like accounting, microeconomics, macroeconomics, project management, human resource, etc. It wasn't until my final year in the college taking a general business program that I finally realized that my passions lied elsewhere. I started teaching myself some Java coding during that time and my interest in technology grew to be something much more. After graduating from business I applied to the Computer Programmer/Analyst program at Conestoga College and now here I am!

When I'm not working on school projects I like to play video games, read, watch movies, play with our cat Houdini, and swim (during the 2 months that Canada gets some sun).
